About us

A face behind Fly Atlas

Or should it be about me? I am running Fly Atlas following my passion of paragliding and Morocco.
I am a French Paragliding Instructor recognised by the British Hangliding and Paragliding Association.

My friends think I am crazy, that I left my Environmental Engineer job to get into paragliding and live & travel in my van named Toto in 2019. Maybe they are not completely wrong but it's part of my adventure!

I LOVE taking pictures and videos. This site is full of my pictures. I have my personal instagram account where I get to post other content than paragliding, like van convertion. You're welcome to follow.
