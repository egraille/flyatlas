Another experience of the Atlas moutains - Paragliding in Morocco

Another experience of the Atlas

Only an hour away from Marrakech, take-off from the mountains of the Atlas range and fly over the Moroccan desert. We will be taking you to different paragliding sites in Morocco - Aguergour, Ait Ourir, Mzouda, Tiz-N-Test et Tafraout.

Following your choice between a tandem flight, guiding services to show you the best paragliding flying spots of Morocco or coaching services to help you to improve your paragliding skills both on the ground and in the air, we offer tailored services to our customers. Please do not hesitate to contact us for a personalised quote.
