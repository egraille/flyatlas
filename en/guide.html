Guide

Lack of blue skies leave you with a winter blues, come a enjoy Moroccan winters when the flying season is over in your country.

You are an autonomous and experienced pilot (or a group) who wish to discover the Atlas range flying a paraglider. Flying sites to discover are Aguergour, Mzouda, Ait Ourir, Tiz-N-Test, Tafraout. 
We offer tailored guiding services following your wish and needs.
Do not hesitate to contact us to discuss about the needed arrangements to suit your travels.
